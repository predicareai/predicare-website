PrediCare exists because too many people in India discover diabetes, heart disease, and cancer only after it's already too late. We're changing that.

Our Mission

India has 77 million people living with diabetes — most don't know it until symptoms force a hospital visit. The same is true for hypertension, fatty liver, and early-stage cancers.

We believe that if you can predict disease 2–5 years before symptoms appear, you can prevent it. PrediCare is the AI platform that makes that possible — affordable, in Hindi, and built for Indian health patterns.

Our goal: prevent 10 million premature deaths in India by 2035 — starting with Bihar, where 130 million people have among the least access to preventive healthcare of any Indian state.

Our Core Values

Indian genetics, diet patterns, and lifestyle factors are different. Our models are trained on Indian data, available in Hindi, and work offline in low-connectivity areas.

Our Journey

How we got here

2024 — The Idea
The conversation that started everything
Nagendra Kumar, after watching his father receive a dual diagnosis of Type 2 diabetes and heart disease that a doctor said could have been prevented 3 years earlier, commits to building the AI prevention platform India doesn't have yet.
2025 — Research & Foundation
Strategy, prototype, and regulatory pathway
Risk models benchmarked against 50M+ records from public health research consortia (PIMA, UCI, UK Biobank). Working MVP prototype built. CDSCO Class B medical device pathway identified. Seed round opened.
2026 — Launch
predicare.ai goes live
We launch the free health risk assessment publicly. First 500 waitlist users get 3 months of Pro free. Pilots begin with corporate HR teams in Bihar and the Hindi belt.
2027 — Scale
Hindi belt expansion → pan-India
Scale from Bihar across the full Hindi belt: UP, Rajasthan, MP, Jharkhand — 500M+ people. Then pan-India. 10 Indian languages. Insurance partnerships. Target: 1 million users across Tier 2 and Tier 3 India — the market every competitor ignores.
