💚 Our Story

We believe
prevention
is the cure.

PrediCare AI exists because 3.5 billion people have no access to quality healthcare — not because medicine doesn't exist, but because the system never warned them in time.

"Our mission is to give every person on Earth — regardless of income, location or language — the knowledge to protect their own health before it's too late."
Why We Started

The system treats.
We predict.

The world has spent trillions building hospitals, training doctors, and developing medicines. Yet 71% of all deaths globally come from diseases that are largely preventable — diabetes, heart disease, stroke, cancer.

The tragedy isn't that we lack cures. It's that we wait for disease to arrive before we act. By then, it's often too late, too expensive, or too advanced to treat effectively.

PrediCare AI was founded on one idea: the most powerful moment in healthcare is 10 years before the diagnosis. That's when a single piece of information — "your risk is elevated" — can change everything.

We're building the tool that delivers that information to everyone, for free, in their language, on the phone they already own.

How We Work

Our Values

These aren't decorative. They're the decisions we make every day.

01
Access over revenue
The free tier will always exist. We will never paywall the core risk assessment. If someone can't afford it, they need it the most. Business model must work around this — not the other way.
02
Users over investors
We listen to patients and doctors first. If an investor wants us to build something that doesn't serve our users, we decline. The product's job is to improve health outcomes — everything else follows.
03
Honest over optimistic
We don't exaggerate our AI's capabilities. We display confidence levels. We always recommend seeing a doctor. We'd rather under-promise and over-deliver than the reverse.
04
Build for Bharat first
Every product decision asks: does this work for a 45-year-old in rural UP with a ₹8,000 phone and patchy internet? If yes, it works everywhere. If no, we redesign it.
