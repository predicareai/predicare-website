Co-Founder Vetting Guide

10 Questions to Ask Every Candidate

Have 3 conversations minimum before deciding. Do a 2-week paid trial project together before signing any equity agreement.

01 — MOTIVATION
"Why health AI, and why now? Have you had a personal experience with the healthcare system failing you or someone close?"
02 — TECHNICAL DEPTH
"Walk me through the hardest production ML problem you've solved. What failed first and how did you fix it?"
03 — RISK TOLERANCE
"What's your runway? How long can you work without salary? Do you have dependents and how does your family feel about this?"
04 — WORKING STYLE
"Describe a time you strongly disagreed with a team decision. What happened and what would you do differently?"
05 — INDIA CONTEXT
"How would you approach building for a 55-year-old woman in Rajasthan with a Class 8 education and a 2G connection?"
06 — EQUITY MINDSET
"What equity are you expecting, and how did you arrive at that number? How do you think about vesting and cliffs?"
07 — SPEED
"How long would it take you to build a functional MVP of the onboarding flow and risk score engine from scratch?"
08 — REFERENCES
"Can I speak with a past manager and a past peer — not just people you're close with, but someone who saw you under pressure?"
09 — DEAL-BREAKERS
"What would make you walk away from this? What are your absolute non-negotiables in a co-founding relationship?"
10 — THE REAL ONE
"If PrediCare fails in 18 months — not succeeds, fails — was it still worth it to you? Why?"
