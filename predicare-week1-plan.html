Day 01
Monday — Foundation Day
START HERE
Set up everything
▶
9 AM
Register predicare.ai domain BUILD
Go to GoDaddy or Namecheap right now. Also register @PrediCareAI on Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Instagram. These handles will be taken if you wait. Cost: ~₹800/year for the domain.
10 AM
Deploy landing page on Vercel LAUNCH CRITICAL
Create a free Vercel account. Connect your GitHub. Upload the landing page HTML you already have. Point your domain to it. You'll have a live website before lunch. This is your single most important task today.
11 AM
Connect Mailchimp waitlist to landing page BUILD
Free Mailchimp account → create an audience → embed the form on your landing page. Every email you collect is a potential user, investor contact, and beta tester. Set up an auto-reply: "You're on the list — we launch in Bihar & the Hindi belt Q3 2026 — Bihar & Hindi belt first, pan-India by Q1 2027."
1 PM
Create LinkedIn company page for PrediCare AI LAUNCH
Use the exact copy from your Brand Kit. Upload the logo, set the tagline, fill out all fields. Then post your FIRST company update: "We're building the AI that predicts disease before it happens. Watch this space." Simple. No overthinking.
2 PM
Set up Twitter/X @PrediCareAI LAUNCH
Copy your bio from the Brand Kit. Upload the logo as profile pic, navy gradient as header. Follow 50 relevant accounts: @KalaariCapital, @BlueVentures, @iSPIRT, @NASSOCOMHealth, and 40+ healthtech founders. Twitter/X is your fastest path to investor attention.
3 PM
Update your personal LinkedIn headline CRITICAL
Change your headline to: "Founder @ PrediCare AI | Building AI that predicts disease before it happens | Preventive Health for India's 1.4B". This alone will start attracting inbound messages. Every person who visits your profile now knows what you're building.
4 PM
Create [email protected] email ADMIN
Set up Google Workspace (₹125/month) with [email protected]. This makes every communication look professional instantly. Use it for all investor and partner outreach — not your personal Gmail.
5 PM
Tell 20 people personally — message them tonight OUTREACH
Message 20 friends, family, and colleagues: "I just launched PrediCare AI — an AI that predicts health risks before symptoms appear. Would mean the world if you signed up and shared: predicare.ai". These first 20 signups are your social proof foundation.
🎯
Day 1 Goal: Live website + 20 signupsBy midnight: predicare.ai is live, all social handles are created, and you have your first 20 email signups. Everything else this week builds on this foundation.
Day 02
Tuesday — The Founder Post
→
Go public
▶
8 AM
Post your founder story on LinkedIn (personal) LAUNCH CRITICAL
Use the founder story post from your Outreach Kit — but personalize it with YOUR real story. The closer it is to your real experience, the more powerful it will be. Post at exactly 8:30 AM IST for maximum weekday reach. Then stay at your desk for 2 hours and reply to every single comment.
9 AM
Post the same story on Twitter/X as a 7-tweet thread LAUNCH
Use the thread template from your Outreach Kit. Schedule Tweets 2–7 to auto-post every 10 minutes using Buffer (free). This keeps the thread active in feeds for 70 minutes — dramatically increasing total reach.
10 AM
DM everyone who liked/commented on your post OUTREACH
"Thanks for the support [Name] — you're exactly who we're building this for. Here's your personal early access link: predicare.ai/early. Would love your honest feedback when you try it." Personalize each message. Target 50+ DMs today.
2 PM
Share in 5 WhatsApp groups OUTREACH
Identify and join: 2 doctor/health professional groups, 1 startup/entrepreneurship group, 1 tech group, 1 college alumni group. Write a personal note — not a generic post. "I've been building something for the past few months and just launched publicly today. Would love your eyes on it." Then share the link.
4 PM
Submit to Product Hunt "Coming Soon" page ADMIN
Product Hunt lets you create a "coming soon" page before launch. Start collecting upvotes and followers. Even 50 followers before your PH launch day will dramatically boost your visibility on launch day itself.
🎯
Day 2 Goal: 100–200 waitlist signupsYour founder post should generate significant traffic. 100 signups by end of day is achievable. Reply to every LinkedIn comment — the algorithm will keep showing your post to new people for 48 hours.
Day 03
Wednesday — Doctor Outreach
→
Build credibility
▶
9 AM
Identify 20 doctors on LinkedIn in Patna + Hindi belt OUTREACH
Search LinkedIn: "General Physician Patna", "Cardiologist Patna", "Physician Bihar". Find 20 doctors with active LinkedIn profiles and 500+ connections. They are opinion leaders — 1 doctor endorsement = 200 patients.
10 AM
Comment thoughtfully on 10 doctors' recent posts OUTREACH
Before DMing, engage first. Leave a 2–3 sentence intelligent comment on their most recent posts. "This is exactly the kind of preventive thinking we need more of in India. The gap between detection and intervention is costing millions of lives." This warms them before your DM arrives.
11 AM
Post the data-driven LinkedIn post (10 statistics) LAUNCH
Use the "10 numbers" post from your Outreach Kit. This is your second major content piece — it builds authority and is highly shareable among healthcare professionals. Post at 11 AM on Wednesday for midweek peak reach.
2 PM
Start building your CTO co-founder target list BUILD
Spend 2 hours on LinkedIn identifying 15 senior ML engineers at Qure.ai, Niramai, Practo, 1mg, mFine, and Apollo247. Note their names, profiles, and one personal detail about their work. Quality of research here = quality of DMs later.
4 PM
Register for iSPIRT or NASSCOM HealthTech next event ADMIN
Find their next event or webinar at ispirt.in or nasscom.in. Register and add it to your calendar. If there's a Slack/Discord community, join it today. These communities contain future co-founders, advisors, and customers.
🎯
Day 3 Goal: 300 signups + 1 doctor conversation bookedIf even one doctor agrees to a 20-minute feedback call, that's a potential advisor, advocate, and clinical validator rolled into one. Prioritize this above almost everything else today.
Day 04
Thursday — First Investor Emails
→
Start the round
▶
9 AM
Map your LinkedIn 2nd-degree connections to 12 target investors CRITICAL
Go through each of the 12 target investors from your Outreach Kit. Lead with the Bihar-first angle — it is genuinely differentiated. Every other health AI founder says "Bengaluru". You are the only one who can credibly say "I am building for the 130M people in Bihar who every other startup ignores." Search their LinkedIn profiles and look at your shared connections. For each shared connection, note the relationship strength (close friend vs. acquaintance). A warm intro is 10x more valuable than cold email.
10 AM
Request 3–5 warm intro emails from your connectors OUTREACH
Use the warm intro request template from your Outreach Kit. Write the 2-paragraph blurb FOR them so all they need to do is forward it. Start with investors connected to people you trust most. Never ask one person to intro you to more than 2 investors at once — it dilutes their credibility.
11 AM
Send 3 cold emails to bottom-tier investors on your list OUTREACH
Start with your 3 lowest-priority investors first. This is practice — you'll refine your pitch based on how they respond (or don't). Use the cold email template but personalize the fund reference paragraph for each one. Track open rates with a free tool like Mailtrack.
2 PM
Apply to YC Co-Founder Matching platform BUILD
Create your profile at ycombinator.com/cofounder-matching. Fill it out completely — company description, what you're looking for, your background. This is where the most serious technical talent actively looking for co-founder opportunities is concentrated.
4 PM
Send 5 personalized co-founder DMs to ML engineers OUTREACH
Use your 15-person target list from yesterday. Pick the 5 most promising profiles. Personalize each DM with a specific reference to their work. Quality over quantity — these DMs should take 15 minutes each, not 2 minutes each.
🎯
Day 4 Goal: 3 investor emails sent + 5 co-founder DMs sentDon't expect replies yet. You're planting seeds. Your waitlist growth (400+ by now) IS your pitch — reference it in every investor email.
Day 05
Friday — Prototype & Validate
→
Show, don't tell
▶
9 AM
Deploy the MVP prototype to predicare.ai/assess BUILD CRITICAL
You have the interactive health risk assessment prototype from this kit. Deploy it at predicare.ai/assess. Add a banner at the top: "Beta — give us feedback". This is now your most powerful sales tool — it shows investors and users a WORKING product, not just a landing page.
10 AM
Post: "We just launched our beta assessment — try it now" LAUNCH
Post on LinkedIn and Twitter/X with a screenshot of the assessment results screen. "We just launched our beta health risk assessment. Answer 12 questions. Get your personal 5-year disease risk score. It takes 5 minutes and it's free: predicare.ai/assess". Product demos beat all other content for engagement.
1 PM
Conduct 3 user interviews (friends, family, anyone willing) BUILD
Call 3 people. Ask them to share their screen and take the assessment while you watch and listen. Don't tell them what to do — just observe. Note: where they hesitate, what confuses them, what surprises them in the results. 30 minutes of watching someone use your product = 30 days of guessing.
3 PM
Send the assessment link to all 12 investors you've contacted OUTREACH
"Quick update: we just shipped our first working product — a 12-question health risk assessment. 600 people have taken it in 4 days. Would love your thoughts if you have 5 minutes: predicare.ai/assess". This transforms you from "founder with an idea" to "founder shipping product."
🎯
Day 5 Goal: Working beta live + 600 total signups + 3 user interviews doneHaving a live, working product by end of Week 1 is exceptional. Most founders take months to get here. You're doing it in 5 days.

→
Build the audience
▶
10 AM
Post your co-founder search publicly on LinkedIn LAUNCH
Use the co-founder search post template from your Outreach Kit. Post it Saturday morning — LinkedIn reach is lower on weekends, but the people who see it on weekends are often the most serious (developers working on side projects, startup people checking in).

Write your Hacker News "Show HN" post LAUNCH
Post on HN: "Show HN: PrediCare AI – Health risk prediction before symptoms, built for India (predicare.ai/assess)". Keep it technical and honest: what you've built, what the problem is, what's working, what's not. HN readers are sophisticated and reward authenticity. Even 50 upvotes = massive reach.
1 PM
Schedule next 2 weeks of social content in Buffer ADMIN
Take your Twitter content calendar from the Brand Kit. Schedule Week 2 and Week 3 tweets in Buffer (free for 10 posts). You should never have an empty social calendar again — consistency signals health and seriousness to investors who are quietly watching your accounts.
3 PM
DM 10 more co-founder candidates + 5 more investors OUTREACH
Keep the pipeline moving. By now you may have 1–2 co-founder responses and 1–2 investor replies. Follow up with new candidates while the early conversations progress. Never have fewer than 10 active co-founder conversations happening simultaneously.
🎯
Day 6 Goal: 800 signups + Hacker News post liveA good HN post can 10x your growth in a single day. Prepare honest, technical answers for the comments — HN users will ask hard questions about your ML model accuracy, data sources, and regulatory plan. Be ready.
Day 07
Sunday — Review & Plan Week 2
→
Reflect & accelerate
▶
10 AM
Review all 7-day metrics honestly ADMIN
Waitlist signups? Investor emails sent/replied? Co-founder DMs sent/replied? Doctor conversations? Beta users? Write these numbers down. The gap between what you planned and what happened IS your learning. Don't hide from it — it's your roadmap for Week 2.
11 AM
Write and send Week 1 update email to your waitlist LAUNCH
"Hi [Name], one week in — here's what we've built, what we've learned, and what's coming next." Share your signup numbers, a screenshot of the assessment, 1 user quote, and a preview of Week 2. This is your first "investor update" — write it as if investors are reading it (they might be).
1 PM
Identify your 3 biggest Week 1 learnings BUILD
What surprised you? What worked better than expected? What failed completely? Write 3 honest paragraphs. These learnings ARE your Week 2 strategy. The founders who improve fastest are the ones who reflect most honestly — not the ones who work the longest hours.
3 PM
Book your first 3 investor intro meetings for Week 2 OUTREACH
By now you should have 1–3 investor responses. Book Calendly slots for Week 2. If you have zero responses, that's data — your subject line or opening paragraph needs to change. A/B test 2 different cold email versions in Week 2. Don't assume. Test.
🎯
Day 7 Goal: 1,000 waitlist signups + 1 investor meeting booked + 1 co-founder conversation activeIf you hit these three numbers, Week 1 was a success. If you didn't — the gap tells you exactly what to fix in Week 2. Either way, you're no longer Day 1. You're a founder who has shipped.
