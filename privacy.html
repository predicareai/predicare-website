3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • Providing the service: Running the risk assessment, generating your risk score and prevention plan
  • Personalisation: Remembering your preferences, language, and previous assessments
  • Communications: Sending product updates, waitlist notifications, and responses to your enquiries
  • Improving PrediCare AI: Understanding how people use the product to make it better — using anonymised, aggregated data only
  • Safety & security: Detecting fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our obligations under applicable Indian law
We never use your health data for advertising, insurance risk assessment, employer screening, or any commercial purpose beyond providing and improving PrediCare AI.

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Where We Store Your Data

All personal data is stored on Google Cloud servers located in the Mumbai, India region — ensuring your data stays within Indian jurisdiction and complies with India's data localisation requirements.

5.2 How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: Health data stored in our databases is encrypted using AES-256
  • Access controls: Only authorised PrediCare team members can access production data, with full audit logs
  • Password security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain-text passwords
  • Regular security audits: We conduct security reviews of our systems regularly

9. India's DPDP Act 2023 Compliance

PrediCare AI complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India. Specifically:

  • Lawful basis: We process your personal data on the basis of your consent, which you give when you use our service
  • Data minimisation: We collect only the data necessary to provide the health risk assessment and prevention recommendations
  • Purpose limitation: Your data is used only for the purposes described in this policy
  • Data localisation: All personal data is stored in India (Google Cloud Mumbai)
  • Data Principal rights: You can exercise all rights granted to Data Principals under the DPDP Act by contacting [email protected]
  • Grievance officer: For DPDP-related complaints, contact our designated officer at [email protected]
Health data classification: Your health and lifestyle data may be classified as "sensitive personal data" under applicable Indian law. We treat it accordingly with the highest level of protection.
