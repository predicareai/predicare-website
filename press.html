About PrediCare AI

PrediCare AI is India's first AI-powered preventive health platform built specifically for the Hindi belt and underserved India. Founded in 2026 by Nagendra Kumar — a healthcare IT professional who spent years watching patients in Bihar arrive with preventable diseases that had gone undetected for years — PrediCare uses AI risk models to predict an individual's 5-year risk of diabetes, cardiovascular disease, and other conditions, years before symptoms appear. The platform is Hindi-first, offline-capable, DPDP Act 2023 compliant, and priced at ₹0 to start. PrediCare is launching in Bihar in Q3 2026 and expanding pan-India by Q1 2027. predicare.ai
