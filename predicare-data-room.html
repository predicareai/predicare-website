Why India First

India has the world's largest preventable disease burden: 77M diabetics (most undiagnosed), 1.5M annual heart attack deaths, and a 1:1,511 doctor-to-patient ratio. ABDM gives PrediCare a regulatory moat no foreign competitor can replicate. India is not just a market — it's our proprietary data flywheel.

The ABDM Moat

Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission gives PrediCare legal access to longitudinal patient health records across 1.4B Indians — with consent. No competitor (Ada, Babylon, mFine) has this. ABDM integration means our AI trains on real Indian health data, not Western datasets biased toward different populations.
