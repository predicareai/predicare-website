Pricing FAQ
Will the free plan always be free? ▾
Yes — permanently. The core 5-disease risk assessment will always be free. Our mission is access for everyone, not just premium users.
What does "Beta — 3 months free" mean? ▾
Early users who join the waitlist now get the first 3 months of Premium completely free when we launch. No credit card needed during beta.
Is my health data safe? ▾
Yes. AES-256 encrypted, stored in India (Google Cloud Mumbai), and never sold to any third party — not insurers, not advertisers, not anyone.
Is this a medical diagnosis? ▾
No. PrediCare AI provides health risk indicators — not a medical diagnosis. Always consult a qualified doctor for health decisions.
