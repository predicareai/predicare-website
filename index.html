Pricing FAQ
Will the free plan always be free? ▾
Yes — permanently. The core 5-disease risk assessment and basic prevention plan will always be free. Our mission is access for everyone, not just premium users. Business revenue comes from Premium subscriptions and Clinic plans.
What does "Beta — 3 months free" mean? ▾
Early users who join the waitlist now will get the first 3 months of Premium completely free when we launch. No credit card needed during beta. After 3 months, you can choose to continue at ₹299/month or stay on the free plan.
Is my health data safe? ▾
Yes. Your health data is encrypted end-to-end using AES-256, stored in India (Google Cloud Mumbai), and never sold to any third party — not insurers, not advertisers, not anyone. We comply with India's DPDP Act 2023. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I cancel the Premium plan anytime? ▾
Yes — cancel anytime with no penalty. Your account reverts to the Free plan and you keep all your historical data. No questions asked.
Is this a medical diagnosis? ▾
No. PrediCare AI provides health risk indicators and awareness — not a medical diagnosis. It's a preventive wellness tool. For any health decisions, always consult a qualified doctor. When your risk score is high, we connect you to verified doctors near you.
